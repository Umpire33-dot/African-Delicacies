Making pounded yam and egusi

Here's is a simple and common method of making pounded yam and egusi:

Pounded yam:

Making pounded yam is quite easy, all you have to do is peel and boil the yam without adding salt or spices. Once it's soft you process the hot chunks until smooth using a food processor. Before that has always been done using mortal and pestel which is quite stressful and takes a lot of time but now we've appliances that can help us do that while we prepare our Egusi soup

Egusi soup: Making is quite tricky and may not get desired taste we want if not prepared right but we'll try to make that easy for you. Now all you need to is saute onions in palmoil, add your blended melon seeds, stock, crayfish and precooked meats. Simmer until oil rises, stir in crayfish, pepper and greens.Let it simmer for some mins, serve hot and Voila! you have your Pounded-yam and Egusi ready to be enjoyed with chilled palmwine.

And that's how you prepare your Pounded yam and Egusi
