Pounded-Yam and Pamwine

Pounded yam originates from West Africa, especially Nigeria, Ghana, and Benin, where yam has been cultivated for thousands of years. Pounded yam is a food considered favorite of many tribes and cultures across West Africa, like the Benue and Ekiti people of Nigeria who use it in celebrating the New Yam's festival with Egusi soup, a melon soup almost as old as Pounded yam itself.
